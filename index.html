This site is a UX prototype. The state-by-state guidance, response deadlines, fees, citations, exemption lists, sample request language, and contacts are placeholder text. None of it has been verified, and much of it is wrong or out of date.

Don't use this site to write a public records request, judge whether a denial is valid, or decide how to appeal. For accurate, state-specific information, use a verified resource like the Reporters Committee Open Government Guide.
